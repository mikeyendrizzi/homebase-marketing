How does the offer process work?
🏡
Once you find a home you love, your agent submits a written offer with your price and terms. The seller can accept, reject, or counter. If accepted, you move into escrow.
What's earnest money?
🏡
Earnest money is a deposit (usually 1-3% of the purchase price) that shows the seller you're serious. It goes toward your down payment at closing.
